### Full Description
Play the classic XOX (tic-tac-toe) game in a clean and fast interface.

Challenge a friend on the same device and enjoy a smooth two-player experience.
Track wins for Player X and Player O, keep draw counts, and celebrate every victory with animated confetti.

Features:
- Two-player local gameplay (X vs O)
- Live scoreboard for X wins, O wins, and draws
- Winning celebration animation with confetti
- Quick restart for a new round
- Reset score option for fresh matches
- Lightweight app with simple, responsive design

Whether you want a quick game break or a best-of series with friends, XOX Game is easy to pick up and fun to replay.
